Our Story

"I've always known I wanted to have a business of my own."

— Nadia Cutillo, Founder

The Bean Espresso Bar is the vision of Nadia Cutillo — an entrepreneur originally from Toronto, proudly rooted in Italian heritage, who has built a refined and radically welcoming café on Wilton Drive in the heart of Wilton Manors, Florida. What began as a lifelong dream is now a living expression of culture: every cup pulled, every pastry made in-house, every corner of the space designed to slow you down and make you feel at home.

The Bean channels the quiet ritual of the classic Italian caffè — where espresso is not a transaction but a pause, where the bar is a gathering point, where warmth is built into the architecture of the day — and brings it to one of South Florida's most vibrant and inclusive communities. This is not a chain. It is not a formula. It is one person's heritage, made into a place for everyone.
